-------SNOWMAN HUNT---------

*This game, snowman hunt, is a simple game where a snowman tries to hit some targets to fetch points.
*For each target that the snowman hits, it get some points.
*There is an obstacle in between which moves vertically, which has to be dodged.
*You can regulate the speed of your projectile as you wish.

----CONTROLS----
*To move cannon up: 'w'
*To move cannon down: 's'
*To shoot projectile: 'space'. The more you press 'space' key, the more speed your projectile gets.
*To zoom in: 'z';
*To zoom out: 'x';
